Lesson 1: Introduction to Chess

In this lesson, you'll learn the basics of chess, including how the pieces move and how to set up the board.

Piece Movement:

  • Pawn: Move one square forward (two squares on the first move).
  • Rook: Move in a straight line (vertically or horizontally).
  • Bishop: Move diagonally.
  • Knight: Move in an "L" shape.
  • Queen: Move in any direction (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally).
  • King: Move one square in any direction.
